Terms & Conditions of User Finance

1. One-Time Activation Fee

An activation fee of KES 500.00 is required to activate your account. This fee pays for server resources and initializes your invite link. All activation fees are final and non-refundable.

2. Invite Commissions

Commissions are earned up to 3 levels (Level 1: KES 250, Level 2: KES 100, Level 3: KES 50). These commissions are credited automatically when your referrals pay their activation fee. Unactivated referrals will not generate any commissions.

4. Withdrawal Rules

The minimum withdrawal limit is KES 50.00. Withdrawal requests are processed chronologically. Mobile carrier transaction fees apply where necessary.
